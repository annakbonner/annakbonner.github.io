HELLO, I'M ANNA!

Welcome to my site! I hand-coded this page to serve as a virtual portfolio to showcase my in-class projects and hands-on experience in the design world! Thank you so much for visiting!

I grew up in Northern Virginia, right outside Washington DC and am a student at James Madison University. I am studying in the school of Media Arts and Design and am pursuing a concentration in Interactive Design. Additionally, I am working on minors in General Business and Art.
My goal in life is to impact the world in a positive way through my creativity. “It has been said that something as small as the flutter of a butterfly’s wing can ultimately cause a typhoon halfway around the world.” – Chaos Theory

Outside of school, some of my hobbies include drawing, reading, traveling, and being outdoors. Most importantly, I work hard to positively impact those around me and hope to continue to do so in whatever career path I take. For more information about me, take a look at my resume below!
